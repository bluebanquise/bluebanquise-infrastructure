Algoric


Algoric is 'amatory' and 'science fans' project, leaded by the Fab Lab La Fabrique du Loch, which makes it a human scale project.

Our goals are the following:
  • Provide HPC resources to other FabLabs (mostly blender farm, video/image processing, hacking, etc.)
  • Provide HPC resources and training to local enterprises (mostly sails simulations) and universities.
  • Teach how IT can be fun to nearby schools, and provide general IT culture courses.
  • Being an independent HPC center, able to answer exotic demands.
  • Re-use old HPC equipment, with a green approach (PUE < 1 by using heat to warm up Fab Lab building).

The Algoric team specially thanks CINES who provided us our first re-used cluster.
